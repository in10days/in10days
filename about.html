about our journey

At KARA Studios, we envision the compelling power of short stories. The company has its core values lying in the narratives being shared and spread through its platform. Why would a story be called a story if not talked about and spread? Whether it's the whispered tales from our grandparents, the compelling narratives of our day to-day, or the imaginative worlds waiting to be explored, short stories connect us. Rooted deeply in the diverse culture of India and the families, we've created a space where these tales are not just told but celebrated.

  • Founded by : Karan Kailash Murarka
  • Founded in: Oct 2011
  • Partnerships with: Google, Facebook, Hotstar, Yahoo & SIFY
  • Number of channels on YouTube: 65+
  • Viewership of across all content:4.5 Billion+
  • Content type: Animation, Original Content & Daily News

About the Founder

Karan Murarka standing at the hail of the Digital Industry put up a concrete task of bringing the storytelling art to its peak. With a Degree in Computer Engineering & MBA in Retail from Welingkar Institute of Management, Karan started his journey in the Entertainment business with Reliance Entertainment. Prior to Reliance he was with Bombay Stock Exchange for 3 years and learnt the online nitty-gritties by working in the IT and Systems of BSE.
